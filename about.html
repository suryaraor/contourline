About Us

We are a bunch of engineers based in the cyber city of Hyderabad, India, who have amassed a wealth of expertise and experience during our careers spanning over several years in a couple of high-tech industries. Tech-savvy youth, who in the due course realized how vital manpower is for an engineering industry to survive and grow. That’s how and why Contourline happened. We had been envisioning eco-systems where recruitment, staffing and placing would be impeccable enough to lead to the effortless merging of technology and people using it.

Once start-ups, our success in inducting the right professionals at right roles has seen us go upscale by entering uncharted landscapes. Today Contourline has made its mark as a game-changer by successfully catering to the staffing requirements of some European blue chips thus leaving our footprints in the global arena. As the name suggests, we are here to change the contours of organizations, beginning from the scratch, that’s people.
